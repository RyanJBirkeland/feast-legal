Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

Feast (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is a meal planning app that helps you create personalized meal plans, track nutrition, and manage grocery lists. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account info: Email address (for authentication via Supabase Auth)
  • Health & dietary preferences: Health goals, dietary restrictions, food sensitivities, health conditions, activity level, cooking skill — provided during onboarding
  • Usage data: Meal plans created, recipes saved/cooked/rated, grocery lists
  • Device info: Push notification tokens (optional, for reminders)
  • Diagnostics: Crash and performance data (via Sentry) to keep the app stable

How We Use Your Data

  • Generate personalized meal plans using AI (Anthropic)
  • Provide nutrition analysis aligned to your health goals
  • Power the cooking assistant with context about your preferences
  • Improve recipe recommendations based on your engagement
  • Send optional push notifications (meal reminders, weekly reviews)
  • Diagnose crashes and performance issues

AI & Third-Party Services

We use Anthropic’s API to power meal planning, nutrition analysis, and cooking assistance. Your dietary preferences and recipe interactions are sent to Anthropic to generate personalized responses. Anthropic does not use this data to train their models (per their API data usage policy).

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (hosted on AWS). We use row-level security (RLS) to ensure you can only access your own data. Authentication is handled via Supabase Auth with secure token management.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic — dietary preferences for AI-powered features (no direct identifiers)
  • Supabase — data storage and authentication
  • Instacart — grocery-list items, only when you choose to send a list
  • Sentry — crash and performance diagnostics

Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data from Settings
  • Export: Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications at any time

Children’s Privacy

Feast is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
