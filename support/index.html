Common questions

How do I create a meal plan?
Open the Plan tab and tap "Build My Plan." The Dietitian builds a personalized week around the preferences you set during onboarding. You can chat to swap meals or adjust portions anytime.
How do I import a recipe?
Go to Add Recipe → "Import from URL" and paste any recipe link, or share a link to Feast from your browser. Feast extracts the recipe for you.
How do I delete my account?
Open Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.
How do I turn notifications on or off?
Settings → Appearance & App Settings → Notifications. You can disable reminders at any time.
Is my data private?
Yes. See our Privacy Policy for exactly what we collect and how it's used. We never sell your data.
